The ROBLOX app

Roblox is an online game platform and game creation system developed by Roblox Corporation that allows users to program and play games created by themselves or other users. The platform hosts user-created games of multiple genres coded. Roblox is free, with in-game purchases available through a virtual currency called Robux.
